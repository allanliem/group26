Paul's Group Feedback

For this assignment we were given the unexpected situation of dealing with a pandemic for the duration of our semester. Dealing with this situation during our group work definitely had an effect on the organization of our group activities. The first challenge was that all communication had to be done over the internet and we were not able to do any of our group work in our scheduled tutes on campus, which is what we expected at the start of the semester.
I think we dealt with this well by utilizing Microsoft Teams for the delegation and discussion of the group ideas and responsibilities. An area I think we could improve would be the scheduling of our meetings; with the current pandemic getting the whole team together for meetings presented a challenge for us as everyone’s schedules had been severely affected. One thing that surprised me was the effectiveness that we were able to organize our group without any face to face contact. We were forced to communicate over the internet but I don’t feel it had too much effect on the effectiveness of our work throughout the assignment. One thing I learned is that group work can be more dynamic than I expected, with the current pandemic situation going on I thought that this project had potential to be very difficult but with the adaptability of our members I think we overcame this well, adaptability was one of the things that our members scored well on our personality tests so it was interesting to see how that came into the group assignment.

Thanks for reading about us!

This has been a brief insight into our lives and interests within IT. Our passions and interests as individuals drives us to learn as a team. Hopefully using the skills we nurture here at RMIT opens up many doors for us in the IT industry.
