Mr Setha Choummanivong

Background Information:

Setha Choummanivong is a Customer Technician at KPMG, a global network of professional services firms providing Audit, Management Consulting, Legal, Digital and Advisory services. KPMG works with renowned companies to help them solve complex challenges and develop.Mr Choummanivong completed a masters of business information system and is currently working towards a career in cloud engineering.

What kind of work does Setha Choummanivong do?

Choummanivong is a Customer Technician with his role including face to face IT support for system applications, audio and video equipment as well as managing IT hardware at the KPMG Melbourne head office. He can typically be found helping with technical problems on services and training non-IT professionals with their computer knowledge. He also tests and troubleshoots computer networks as well as setting up computers and softwares for clients and employees. Setha also provides emergency services in the case of any malware attacks or any issue that affects the company's IT systems.

What kinds of people does Setha Choummanivong interact with?

His role is to provide IT support to the internal employees of the business (KPMG). This includes both regular employees from all departments and some clients of the business. Clients of this business can vary from small businesses to well known companies such as The Australian Department of Defence who seek technical assistance.

Where does Setha Choummanivong spend most of his time?

Most of the time is spent in the office providing onsite support. At times, remote support is required while over the phone in emergency cases or when assisting clients.

What aspect of their position is most challenging?

The most challenging aspect of Mr Choummanivong’s position is being able to communicate with non-technical employees of the business. When attempting to assist a non-IT professional with their technical issues, it can be difficult to try and make the individual understand and learn as they do not have as much knowledge as a professional would. This is even more difficult in circumstances where Mr Choummanivong can not consult a client or employee face to face.

Mr Bryan Llaneza

Background Information:

Bryan Llaneza is a Senior Functional Analyst working for Jemena. This company is renowned for its energy and water transportation assets across the east coast of Australia, supplying millions of Australian households and businesses with these essential services every single day. Mr Llaneza completed a bachelor's degree in computer/information science and held many different positions in the field in order to build his career to where it is now.

What kind of work does Bryan Llaneza do?

Mr Llaneza is a Functional Analyst that supports the Enterprise HR, Payroll and HSE Systems of the Australian company Jemena. This involves configuring the programs to suit the needs of the business, and keeping their systems legally compliant. This also involves liaising with stakeholders and users to keep their systems relevant and current.

What is a functional analyst?

Functional Analysts evaluate a business’s softwares/systems and analyse if it meets the company's needs. When problems are found, a functional business analyst suggests changes to these systems in order to make the company's procedures more efficient. This career path not only requires great technical knowledge, but also communication skills. These individuals work closely with organisations and employees to identity goals and implement changes. Businesses also reach out to Functional Analysts in order to identify and solve any problems that occur with the systems they installed.

What kinds of people does Bryan Llaneza interact with?

Bryan Llaneza works with other IT professionals, internal specialists, business process owners, field staff and auditors. Llaneza has no interactions with the general public while working.

Where does Bryan Llaneza spend most of their time?

In the company’s Melbourne office located in Collins street. Due to most of Llaneza’s work focused on the internal goals within the company, it is not required for him to work anywhere other than the organisation's head office.

What aspect of Bryan Llaneza’s position is most challenging?

The most challenging aspect of Bryan Llaneza’s position is ensuring that new projects work seamlessly with the company’s existing IT Infrastructure. The main objective of his position is to assist the company in achieving their goals, therefore it is essential that Llaneza ensures that this is possible through the use of IT resources. This at times may be challenging due to multiple reasons such as the introduction of new softwares, malwares and making sure they are keeping their systems legally compliant.
